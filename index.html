How to play Mastermind
Object of the Game

In this version of Mastermind, the computer will be the codemaker and you will be the codebreaker. The computer picks a random sequence of 4 pegs. The object of the game is to guess the exact positions of the colors in the computer's sequence in as few guesses as possible. After each guess, the computer gives you a score of exact and partial matches.

Rules
  1. The sequence can contain pegs of colors: red, blue, yellow, green, orange, purple.
    2.
  2. A color can be used any number of times in the sequence.
    3.
  3. All four pegs of the secret sequence will contain a color - no blanks/empties are allowed.
    4.
  4. Each guess must consist of 4 peg colors - no blanks.
    5.
  5. You have 8 guesses to find the secret sequence.
    6.
  6. At the end of each guess, for every peg of correct color in the correct position you will get a red dot.
    7.
  7. At the end of each guess, for every peg of correct color in the wrong position you will get a white dot.
    8.
